Xu Chen

I am a Research Scientist in the AR Perception group at Google. I am interested in 3D digital humans, including their animation, reconstruction and generation. I have been working on neural implicit representations and generative models to enable high-fidelity 3D human reconstruction and animation.

I received my PhD degree from the CLS program with ETH Zurich and Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems, where I was advised by Prof. Otmar Hilliges and co-supervised by Prof. Michael J. Black and Prof. Andreas Geiger. Prior to that, I received my master's degree in robotics from ETH Zurich, and my bachelor's degree in electrical engineering from University of Stuttgart and Wuhan University.
